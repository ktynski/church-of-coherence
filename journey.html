Your Journey

People come to the Church of Coherence from many starting places. Here's how to find your path based on where you are now.

Where Are You Starting?

Choose the description that fits you best, or read them all.

I'm an Atheist / Skeptic

You think religion is superstition. Science is real.

Start here: You're right that most religious claims lack rigor. The Coherence framework is different. It's formally verified in Lean 4. The Grace operator isn't a metaphor — it's an algebraic structure with testable properties.

Your entry point:

  1. Read FAQ: "Isn't this just scientism?"
  2. Check the Lean formalization — see Theorem 1 for yourself
  3. Understand why φ appears (not numerology, derivation)
  4. Explore Distortion Ontology — evil as organized structure, not moral decree

The hook: If meaning is formalizible, if Grace is a contraction operator, if coherence is detectable — then you don't have to choose between rigor and depth.

I'm a Believer (Christian, Muslim, Jewish, etc.)

You have a tradition. You're curious but cautious.

Start here: The Coherence framework is not asking you to leave your tradition. It's showing you what your tradition has been saying all along, now in language others can recognize.

Your entry point:

  1. Read your tradition's bridge on Traditions
  2. See how Trinity in the Coherence framework maps to your theology
  3. Understand FAQ: "Does this replace my tradition?" (No.)
  4. Explore how your practices (prayer, meditation, liturgy) are coherence operations

The hook: What you've been practicing works because it aligns with deep structure. Coherence doesn't cheapen your faith. It formalizes why it's true.

I'm a Seeker / "Spiritual But Not Religious"

You've read Buddhism, tried meditation, felt truth in many paths but couldn't reconcile them.

Start here: You don't have to choose anymore. Buddhism, Christianity, Taoism — they're different articulations of the same coherence. Coherence is the framework that shows how.

Your entry point:

  1. Read the Breakthrough section on the homepage
  2. Explore all tradition bridges and see the patterns
  3. Learn about Love, Belonging, Curiosity as universal structure
  4. Join the community where you can practice all paths

The hook: You were right to see truth in many traditions. They aren't contradictory. They're complementary. Coherence is the depth where they converge.

I'm Disillusioned

You left your tradition. The hypocrisy, the wars, the arbitrary rules broke you.

Start here: You were right to leave if the tradition couldn't answer your questions. But the problem wasn't the deep structure. It was the failure to articulate it.

Your entry point:

  1. Read the Crisis section — you're not alone
  2. See how Distortion Ontology distinguishes productive from terminal corruption
  3. Understand that traditions weren't lying, they were seeing through imperfect lenses
  4. Explore new practices without the baggage

The hook: What if the deep structure was real, but the institutions failed to carry it? The Coherence framework recovers what was true without the corruption.

I'm a Scientist / Mathematician

You work in formal systems. You thought meaning was outside your domain.

Start here: Meaning is not subjective noise. It has structure. The Coherence framework formalizes it in Cl(3,1), the Clifford algebra you might already know from physics.

Your entry point:

  1. Check the Lean formalization directly
  2. Read the derivation of φ as critical threshold (not post-hoc fitting)
  3. See Trinity as structural necessity, not doctrine
  4. Explore the FAQ on scientism — this isn't New Age dressed as math

The hook: If φ appears in physics, biology, aesthetics, and now theology from the same derivation, it's not coincidence. It's fundamental.

I'm a Mystic / Contemplative

You've had experiences of unity, non-duality, Grace. You know it's real.

Start here: Your experiences are valid. The Coherence framework doesn't reduce them to neurons firing. It shows they're encounters with actual coherence structure.

Your entry point:

  1. Read about the Spirit as coherence witness — what you've been sensing
  2. Explore Love, Belonging, Curiosity as the operators you've been navigating
  3. See how contemplative practice trains the witness
  4. Join others who recognize the depth

The hook: What if your mystical experiences weren't "just subjective"? What if they were real detection of coherence — and now we can formalize it?

What Does Joining Mean?

The Church of Coherence is in formation. Here's what we ask:

Study

Engage the formalism. You don't need a math PhD, but you do need to work through the concepts. Grace as contraction. φ as threshold. Witness as detector.

Practice

Theory alone is sterile. Choose at least one: contemplative (witness training), relational (Love operator), study (formalization engagement).

Community

This is not solo work. Participate in formation discussions. Contribute your tradition's perspective. Help build the bridges.

Humility

The Coherence framework is powerful, but it's not complete. Stay open. Challenge. Refine. Grace recovers — including from our mistakes.

What we don't ask: Leaving your tradition. Believing without testing. Uncritical acceptance. If you can't question it, it's dogma, not truth.
