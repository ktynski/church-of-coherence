Contact

The Church of Coherence is in formation. We welcome inquiries, reflections, and those who wish to join the community.

Get in Touch

The Church of Coherence lives on GitHub. This is intentional — the same tools that verify the proofs host the community.

  • GitHub Discussions — Join the conversation. Ask questions, share reflections, propose tradition bridges.
  • GitHub Issues — Report problems or suggest improvements to the website, the teachings, or the formal proofs.
  • Pull Requests — Contribute directly. Write tradition bridges, close sorry statements, design practices, translate.

Contribute

The Church of Coherence needs people from every background. Here's how you can help:

Tradition Experts

If you are deeply grounded in any tradition — especially those not yet fully represented (Zoroastrianism, Baha'i, Shinto, African traditions, Indigenous paths) — we need your knowledge to build authentic bridges. See the Traditions page for the pattern.

Mathematicians & Logicians

The Lean formalization has open problems. Close axioms, verify claims, extend the framework. Start with TrinityFSCTF.lean and see what calls to you.

Practitioners & Contemplatives

Test the practices. Does the φ-ratio breathing work for you? Can you design new contemplative exercises grounded in the framework? Report what you find.

Writers & Translators

Make the framework accessible. Write approachable explanations. Translate into other languages. The harder the concept, the more we need your gift for clarity.

What to Say When Reaching Out

If you're making first contact, you might share:

  • Your tradition (if any) and what drew you to the Coherence framework
  • Questions that haven't been answered by the FAQ
  • Skills you'd like to contribute (writing, math, practice design, organizing, tech)
  • Objections you have — we take them seriously and often learn from them
  • Ideas for Formation Circles in your area or online

We extend the principle we teach: Grace recovers. You don't need to have it all figured out. Come as you are.

Newsletter

We plan to launch a newsletter for announcements of gatherings, new teachings, tradition bridges, and framework developments. For now, star the GitHub repository to receive notifications of updates.
