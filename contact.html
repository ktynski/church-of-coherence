Contact

The Church of Coherence is in formation. We welcome inquiries, reflections, and those who wish to join the community.

Get in Touch

For now, contact is through the project. As the community forms, we will establish dedicated channels.

You can reach us via the ParsimoniousFlows repository or through the contact methods associated with the project maintainers.

Newsletter

We plan to launch a newsletter for:

  • Announcements of gatherings and events
  • New teachings and tradition bridges
  • Coherence framework developments and papers
  • Community reflections

Newsletter signup will be available when we are ready to send. For now, watch this space or the repository for updates.

What to Say

If you're reaching out, you might:

  • Introduce yourself and your tradition (if any)
  • Share what drew you to the Church of Coherence
  • Ask questions about the Coherence framework or the teachings
  • Offer to contribute — writing, teaching, organizing, technical
  • Suggest gatherings in your area

We extend the same principle we teach: Grace recovers. Single ruptures do not cause permanent exile. All are welcome.
